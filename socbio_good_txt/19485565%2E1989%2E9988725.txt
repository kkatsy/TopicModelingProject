Eighth International Congress of Human Genetics

The 8th International Congress of
Human Genetics, sponsored by the
American Society of Human Genetics,
will be held in Washington, D.C., October 6-11, 1991. The first formal announcement is scheduled for late in
1989. The Executive Program Committee is considering topics, speakers and
special events.

Individuals or organizations who anticipate developing pre- or postCongress events, as well as those who
anticipate a need for working meetings
during the week of the Congress, are
urged to write no later than early October, 1989. Contact John J. Mulvihill,
M.D., Secretary-General, ICHG, 9650
Rockville Pike, Bethesda, MD 20814,
USA.
