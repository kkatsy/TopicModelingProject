Annual Business Meeting of the American

Eugenics Society

The 1971 annual business meeting of the
American Eugenics Society will be held at
4:30 p.m., Friday, November 19, following

the conclusion of the Friday afternoon session of the symposium on “Human Evolution: Past, Present and Future.”

Symposium on Human Evolution: Past,

Present, and Future

The American Eugenics Society and the
American Museum of Natural History are
cosponsoring a symposium on “Human
Evolution: Past, Present, and Future’ at
the American Museum of Natural History,
New York City, November 18-20, 1971.
The following is the daily schedule for the
three day symposium:

Thursday, November 18: ‘“‘Man’s Evolutionary Past”

Friday, November 19: “The Continuing
Evolution of Man”

Saturday, November 20: ‘‘Man’s Evolutionary Future”

A copy of the program for the three day
symposium may be obtained by writing
Dr. Carl Bajema, Secretary, American
Eugenics Society, Biology Department,
Grand Valley State College, Allendale,
Michigan 49401,
