New Information-alerting Service
High Priority Research Subjects

A new information-alerting service that
provides subscribers with weekly bibliographies of the journal literature published on
nearly 100 scientific and technical subjects
with high research priorities has been an
- nounced by the Institute for Scientific In
formation (ISI®). Most of the subjects
covered are in the areas of environmental
science, medicine, biology, and chemistry,
with emphasis on the adverse effects of
pollutants, food additives, pesticides, and
drugs.

The new alerting service is called ASCATOPICS™, It monitors the complete output of the 2,500 most important international scientific and technical journals. The
weekly bibliographies sent to subscribers
list every new article on a given subject
