MINUTES OF THE 1966 ANNUAL MEETING OF THE AMERICAN EUGENICS SOCIETY

The Annual Meeting of the American Eugenics Society was held on Thursday, November 10, 1966, at 12:00 noon. Notice of the meeting was sent to all members. The meeting was held in Room 3101 at 230 Park Avenue, New York, New York.
Mr. Frederick Osborn acted as the Chairman of the Meeting. There were in person or by proxy 125 members present.
The Chairman called the meeting to order and requested Mrs. Louise Reverby to act as secretary of the meeting. The minutes of last year's meeting were read and approved.
The Nominating Committee (Gordon Allen, Frederick Osborn, and Harry Shapiro) presented the following slate of Directors for election to the Class of 1969:
Gordon Allen Theodosius Dobzhansky
H. Bentley Glass Clyde V. Riser Richard H. Osborne Norman Ryder James N. Spuhler

There were no other nominations made from the floor. A motion was made by Frederick Osborn and seconded by Dr. Kirk to accept the nominations as read, and the Chairman was instructed to cast one ballot for the proposed slate.
It was unanimously voted to approve the election of F. Clarke Fraser and Richard C. Lewontin as Directors, who were appointed by the Board of Directors at their May 5, 1966 meeting.
The Treasurer's Report was approved and accepted and ordered to be made a part of the minutes of the Meeting.
The Secretary reported on the Society's Third Princeton Conference on Population Genetics and Demography which was held October 20-22 at the Princeton Inn, Princeton, New Jersey. The Conference was attended by demographers, geneticists, psychologists, and anthropologists. Summary of the Conference will appear in the Eugenics Quarterly.
There being no further business to discuss, the Chairman adjourned the Meeting.
Respectfully submitted,
LOUISE REVERBY
Secretary of the Meeting
