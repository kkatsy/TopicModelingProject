A POETIC APOLOGY

Sent at the Last Moment BY a BioloAical Contributor WHO had failed to send the PROMISED REVIEWS
PROEM

Stung By your cutting remarks, Your sarcasm,
Your scathing comments,
And what not,
I,
Burning
For the vindication Of my reputation, And full
Of righteous indignation,
And also,
In sheer self-defence
Did Last night,
Most manfully

Set my shoulder to the wheel, Gather moss, Make a stitch in timeOf which unwonted, Unforeseen, Unforeseeable, AndNota-beneAltogether admirable Activity, I herewith enclose, For your edification, Delectation, Mollification And Purification, The End Result

ENVOI

But Is assured.

Pray do not,

For,

Erroneously,

In all probability,

Attribute the cause ofthis phenomenon And judging

To By the usual interpretations

A propressive mutation,

Of correlations

Nor see in it

And valuations

An indication

Of equations,

That the salvation

It is merely

Of this generation

A sport.

At least this Is the only theory That may be offered Tentatively, Pending further evidence

REFRAIN
And more careful examination Or cautious investigation Of The problem.
