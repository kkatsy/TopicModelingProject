Communications Theodosius Dobzhansky Memorial Fund

The Society for the Study of Human Evolution is instituting the Theodosius Dobzhansky Memorial Prize, to be awarded annually to a young scientist for support of field or laboratory research in evolution. Contributions (tax exempt) are being solicited by a committee of Professor

Dobzhansky's friends. Checks should be made payable to the Society for the Study of Evolution and mailed to the Theodosius Dobzhansky Memorial Committee, c/o Professor Howard Levine, Room 619 Mathematics, Columbia University, New York, N. Y. 10027.
