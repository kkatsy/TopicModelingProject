A symposium concerned with differential reproduction inindividuals with mentaland physical disorders will be held November 13-14, 1970in Caspary Auditorium, The Rockefeller University, New York. The symposium will besponsored by the American Eugenics Society and the Bio-Medical Division of the Population Council. Organizers are: Dr. L. Erlenmeyer-Kimling,

New York State Psychiatric Institute; Dr. Sheldon J. Segal, Population Council (Bio-Medical Division); and Dr. Irving I. Gottesman, University of Minnesota. Further information may be obtained by writing Dr. Irving I. Gottesman, Department of Psychology, Elliott Hall, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, Minnesota 55455.

Eugenics Society of Argentina Celebrates Twenty-fifth Anniversary

The Sociedad Argentina de Eugenesia celebrates the twenty-fifth anniversary of its founding onAugust 4, 1970. The central theme of meetings to beheld in the Universidad del Museo Social Argentino, Buenos Aires, August 1-8, will be "Contemporary Dehumanization--Causes, Effects, and Hu-

man Réévaluation." Interested persons may write to the Secretary General of the Organizing Committee, Prof. Lie. E. H. Stella Diez Ruiz, Universidad del Museo Social Argentino, Corrientes 1723, Buenos Aires, Argentina.
