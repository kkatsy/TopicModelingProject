The Society for the Study of Social Biology Announces the New Editors of the Journal
The Officers and Board of Directors of the Society for the Study of Social Biology are pleased to announce the appointment of Arthur Falek, Georgia Mental Health Institute, Atlanta, Georgia, as the new Editor of Social Biology beginning with the prese.nt issue.
Assisting Dr. Falek are two new Associate Editors: Nancy Howell, Division of Social Sciences, Scarborough College, University of Toronto, West Hill, Ontario, Canada; and Leonard L. Heston, Department of Psychiatry, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, Minnesota.
The new Book Review Editor is Thomas J. Bouchard, Jr., Department of Psychology, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, Minnesota.
