San Diego Biomedical Symposium

The San Diego Biomedical Symposium will beheld February 5-7, 1975, a t the Hilton Inn on Mission Bay, San Diego, California. The symposium will continue last year's topic "Innovations inBiomedicine."
Organized in 1960 as theSan Diego Symposium for Biomedical Engineering, the annual symposium has traditionally

focused on interdisciplinary topics in medical, biological, andengineering technologies. The primary objective is to stimulate interaction among life and physical scientists and practicing physicians.
Further information can be obtained by writing Dr. L. Rosen, Program Chairman, Dept. of Radiology, University of California, Box 109, La Jolla, California 92037.
