THE EUGENICS SOCIETY

THE ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING
was held in the Rooms of the Royal Society,
Burlington House, London, W.1, on Tuesday, May 16th, 1939, at 4 p.m.

In the absence of the President, Mr.
B. S. Bramwell occupied the Chair.

The General Secretary read the notice
convening the meeting.

MinuTEs. The minutes of the Annual
General Meeting held on Tuesday, May
24th, 1938, being contained in the Annual
Report, were taken as read and signed by
the Chairman as correct.

ANNUAL Report. The Chairman, on
behalf of the Council, presented the Annual
Report and outlined the main activities of
the Society in the past year. After certain
questions had been answered, the Report
was unanimously adopted.

- STATEMENT OF ACCOUNTS AND BALANCE
SHEET. The Honorary Treasurer presented
the statement of accounts and balance sheet
for the year ending December 31st, 1938.
The statement and balance sheet were
unanimously adopted.

The Chairman reported that Fellows of the
Society desirous of submitting nominations
to the Council had been duly invited to
apply for nomination papers in the January
1939 issue of the EUGENICS REVIEW. No
Fellows had applied for such papers and no
nominations had been made by persons
outside the Council. The persons nominated
by the Council not being more in number
than the vacancies, it had not been necessary
to hold a ballot. |
